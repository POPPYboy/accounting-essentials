üí° LCM Rule Reminder

  • Inventory must be reported at the lower of cost or market value
  • Market value = Replacement cost (what you'd pay to buy it today)
  • For damaged/obsolete goods, use Net Realizable Value (NRV) - estimated selling price minus disposal costs
  • Write-down entry: Dr. COGS, Cr. Inventory
