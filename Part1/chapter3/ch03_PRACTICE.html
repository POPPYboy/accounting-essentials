Your Mission

It's December 31, and "MediaMax Agency" needs year-end adjusting entries. Identify what needs adjustment and prepare the entries!

Scenario: Identify & Record Adjustments

Review the following situations and prepare the necessary adjusting entries.

Situation 1: Prepaid Insurance

On July 1, the company paid $1,200 for a 12-month insurance policy. No adjustments have been made.

Insurance Expense: $0

Entry: Debit Insurance Expense, Credit Prepaid Insurance

Situation 2: Accrued Salaries

Employees earn $500 per day. The last payday was Friday, Dec 27. Dec 31 is a Tuesday.

Salaries Expense: $0

Entry: Debit Salaries Expense, Credit Salaries Payable

Situation 3: Unearned Revenue

On Nov 1, received $3,000 for 6 months of services in advance. By Dec 31, how much has been earned?

Revenue Earned: $0

Entry: Debit Unearned Revenue, Credit Service Revenue

Summary: Complete Adjusting Journal

Record all three adjusting entries in the journal below.

Date Account Debit Credit
Dec 31 Insurance Expense
Prepaid Insurance
Dec 31 Salaries Expense
Salaries Payable
Dec 31 Unearned Revenue
Service Revenue
