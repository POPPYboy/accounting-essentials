üìö Background & Principles

The recording cycle is systematic and builds on itself. Each step feeds into the next: Source Documents ‚Üí Journal ‚Üí Ledger ‚Üí Trial Balance ‚Üí Financial Statements.

Core Principle: The accounting recording system creates both chronological records (Journal) and categorized summaries (Ledger). This dual perspective ensures accuracy and provides an audit trail for every transaction.
üí° Key Insight: Think of it like building a house. Source documents are your raw materials, the Journal is your blueprint, and the Ledger is your organized storage system. Each foundation is essential for the next layer.
